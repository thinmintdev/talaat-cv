## What shipped for me
- Cursor: refactor assistance on medium repos
- Claude Code: multi-file diffs, architecture planning
- Copilot: inline speed boosts in TS/React

## What didn’t
- Agents that “own” the terminal without tests
- Auto-generated CRUD without auth/validation
